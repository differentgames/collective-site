# The 2019 Different Games @ GDC Ambassadors Program

The Different Games Collective is honored to announce that we have been chosen to participate in **the 2019 Game Developers Conference (GDC) Scholars Program.** We are delighted to share this opportunity with individuals, organizations and institutions that will help support Different Games Collectiveâ€™s mission of fostering diversity and inclusivity in games and games culture.

## About the Program

The DG @ GDC 2019 Ambassadors Program includes passes to GDC, including talks and workshops, as well as one year of access to the GDC Vault. Ambassadors will have the opportunity to network with other members of the 2019 program.
